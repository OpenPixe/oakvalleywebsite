Community Services

Oak Valley vision is to encourage students to become responsible and contributing citizens of the school and within a global context. Such students embrace this aspiration through many and varied community service activities locally and regionally each year.

Co-Curricular Activities

Co – curricular activities in school build character and help develop multiple skills in students. A plethora of activities in literature, art, science, performing arts and sports, benefits students immensely. We also cultivate non-academic skills such as leadership, innovative and entrepreneurship.

Student Council

Student council at Oak is a team of student leaders who work in tandem with teachers to create an effective platform for the student community to explore and discover their interests. It consists of the Head Boy and the Head Girl, Sports Captains, House Captains and Cultural Secretaries who work together and assist in the smooth functioning of the various co-curricular activities of the school. These office bearers set role models to promote leadership for the entire student community. The vital function of the student council is to bring out the talents in the students through various activities and projects organized by the school.

Parent - Teacher Interaction

Oak conducts regular interactions with parents regarding their ward's learning activities, interests, stengths, weaknesses, etc. Parents share the goals they have for their children with teachers to make sure that teachers hold all students to high standards of performance. They are invited to be the part of celebrations !!
